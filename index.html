Design Guidelines

1. Logo Usage

The Industrial Art logo represents the department's identity. It should be used consistently across all media.

2. Clear Space

Maintain a minimum clear space around the logo to ensure visibility and impact. No other elements should encroach upon this space.

3. Colors

Use the primary brand colors. Do not alter the logo colors unless working in monochrome or specific authorized variations.

4. Typography

The official typeface should be used for all accompanying text to maintain brand consistency.
